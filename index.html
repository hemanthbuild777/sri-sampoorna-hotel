Our Story

Sri Sampoorna Hotel is one of the most popular dining destinations in Pedda Amberpet, serving delicious South Indian breakfasts, flavorful biryanis, Chinese dishes, snacks, and beverages. Conveniently located near the ORR exit and Ramoji Film City route, it serves thousands of travelers and local customers every month.
